Recipies

    Plain Punugulu: These are the basic Punugulu that are made with a simple batter of rice and urad dal, seasoned with salt and spices. They are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Masala Punugulu: These Punugulu are made with a spiced batter that includes ingredients such as cumin seeds, coriander seeds, red chili powder, and chopped onions. They have a slightly spicy and savory flavor. Andhra Punugulu: These Punugulu are a specialty of Andhra Pradesh and are made with a batter that includes soaked and ground idli rice, urad dal, and chana dal. They are often served with spicy chutney or sambar. Cheese Punugulu: These Punugulu are made with a batter that includes cheese, onions, and spices. They are a popular variation in restaurants and street food stalls. Sweet Punugulu: These Punugulu are made with a sweet batter that includes jaggery, coconut, and cardamom powder. They are a popular dessert snack and are often served with sweet chutney or honey.
